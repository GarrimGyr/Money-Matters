How do Remittances Change Lives?

Daniel Pratama, McKenzie Humann, John Devine

















In 2020, the countries in the Northern Triangle received billions of dollars in remittances.

Guatemalan families received more than 11 billion dollars in remittances from family members living abroad. This amount is equivalent to 14.6% of Guatemala's GDP.

In the same year, families in El Salvador received nearly 6 billion dollars in remittances from family members living abroad. This amount is equivalent to 24% of El Salvador's GDP.
